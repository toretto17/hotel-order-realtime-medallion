About us

The Collage of Colours is more than a restaurant — it’s where family and friends gather over honest, heartfelt food.

We believe in recipes that have been passed down and dishes that bring people together. Whether you’re here for a quiet meal or a celebration, our kitchen serves food made with care, using fresh ingredients and a touch of tradition. From our grandparents’ favourites to today’s favourites, every plate tells a story.

Come for the flavours, stay for the warmth. We’re here to make your time at the table memorable.
